2 Add it to Gmail

Signatures must be set from Gmail on a computer (not the mobile app).

  1. Open Gmail in your browser and sign in to your Smart Outdoor Equipment account.
  2. Click the gear icon ⚙ in the top-right corner, then click “See all settings.”
  3. On the General tab, scroll down to the Signature section.
  4. Click “+ Create new,” name it “Smart Outdoor,” and click Create.
  5. Click inside the signature edit box, then paste your copied signature (Ctrl + V on Windows, ⌘ + V on Mac).
  6. Under “Signature defaults,” select Smart Outdoor for both “For new emails use” and “On reply/forward use.”
  7. Scroll to the very bottom and click “Save Changes.”
  8. Send yourself a test email to confirm it looks right. If images don’t show, click “Display images below” — they load from the web automatically.
!
